The Best Shows According to Lily

My top 3 shows right now.

Landman

I am invested in all the members of the Norris family. Each of them is different, but I can empathize with each one of them. I think it's because regardless of how flawed they are, they are all good-hearted.

Your Friends and Neighbors

Jon Hamm doesn't seem like a nice person, so it is nice to watch him struggle. Yet, the more I watch it, the more appealing he becomes. Perhaps because he is self-deprecating.

The Last of Us

I thought this season would be boring, especially since Joel gets killed early in the season. I was pleasantly surprised that Ellie and Dina's relationship filled that hole. In the first episode Ellie seems like a simple character full of bravado. In subsequent episode, I realized she is far more complex and interesting.
